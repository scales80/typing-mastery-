Lesson 1: Home Row Mastery

The home row keys (A, S, D, F for the left hand and J, K, L, ; for the right hand) are your foundation. Place your fingers on these keys and use them as your anchor for all other key presses.

  • Keep fingers lightly resting on the home row.
  • Use your index fingers for F and J, guided by the ridges.
  • Return to home row after each key press.

Lesson 2: Build Speed and Accuracy

Start slowly and increase your pace as accuracy improves. Use tools like TypingClub or Keybr.

  • Correct errors as they happen — don’t just keep typing.
  • Focus on posture: sit upright, eyes on the screen, wrists elevated.
  • Practice short timed sessions, 10–15 minutes daily.

Lesson 3: Make It a Habit

Typing is a skill improved with consistency. Track your daily performance and gradually challenge yourself.

  • Use typing games to make practice fun (e.g., NitroType).
  • Use online tests to track WPM (words per minute) progress.
  • Try typing real-world content like emails or notes instead of just drills.
